<p>
  <strong>Offline Map Navigation</strong> is an offline Android navigation app
  powered by <strong>GraphHopper</strong> for routing and <strong>MapLibre</strong>
  for map rendering.
</p>

<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Offline maps</strong> — Access maps without an internet connection.</li>
  <li><strong>Interactive controls</strong> — Rotate, zoom, and toggle 2D/3D compass mode.</li>
  <li><strong>Multi-profile routing</strong> — Optimized for car, motorcycle, and foot.</li>
  <li><strong>Smart recalculation</strong> — Auto-reroutes when you deviate more than 30m.</li>
  <li><strong>Resumable downloads</strong> — Resume interrupted map downloads anytime.</li>
  <li><strong>Fully offline</strong> — No internet required after the initial download.</li>
</ul>
